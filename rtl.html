    RTL Direction

    To use RTL direction set the dir='rtl' in <html> and replace assets/css/sleek.css with assets/css/sleek.rtl.css inside <head>

    
<!-- Rtl Direction -->
<html dir="rtl"></html>

<!-- Stylesheet URL -->
<link id="sleek-css" rel="stylesheet" href="assets/css/sleek.rtl.css">
